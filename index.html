WobblyPaint 95 — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

WobblyPaint 95 ("the App") is a drawing and animation app developed by Chourouk Ben Messaoud. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

No Data Collection

WobblyPaint 95 does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not have user accounts, does not use analytics or advertising services, and does not connect to any external servers.

Photos / Gallery Access

The App requests access to your device's Photos library solely to save drawings, animations, GIFs, and recordings that you choose to export. These files are saved directly to your device and a "WobblyPaint 95" album. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or accessed by the developer or any third party.

Children's Privacy

WobblyPaint 95 does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: chourouk244@gmail.com
