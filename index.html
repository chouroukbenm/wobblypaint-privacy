Privacy Policy for WobblyPaint 95

Last updated: June 15, 2026

WobblyPaint 95 ("the app") is a drawing and animation app developed for iOS, iPadOS, Android, and macOS. This page explains how the app handles your information.

No Data Collection

WobblyPaint 95 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or to any third party. The app works entirely offline. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services of any kind.

Your Drawings and Files

Your drawings, animations, and projects are stored locally on your own device. They are never uploaded anywhere by the app.

Photos / Media Library Access

When you choose to export a drawing, GIF, or screen recording, the app asks for permission to save that file to your device's Photos library (in a "WobblyPaint 95" album). The app may also ask for permission to let you import an existing image from your Photos library to use as a reference or background. These files remain under your control and are never sent to us.

Camera and Location

WobblyPaint 95 does not use your device's camera or location. Any system permission prompts referencing camera or location are inert and not used by any feature of the app.

Children's Privacy

WobblyPaint 95 does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
