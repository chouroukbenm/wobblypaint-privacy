Frequently Asked Questions

Where do my exported drawings and animations go?

When you export a drawing, GIF, or recording, WobblyPaint 95 saves it to your device's Photos app, inside a "WobblyPaint 95" album.

The app can't save to my Photos library

Make sure WobblyPaint 95 has permission to access your Photos. On iOS: Settings > WobblyPaint 95 > Photos > set to "Add Photos Only" or "All Photos". On Android: Settings > Apps > WobblyPaint 95 > Permissions > allow storage/media access.
