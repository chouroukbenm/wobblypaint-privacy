Frequently Asked Questions

Where do my exported drawings, GIFs, and recordings go?

When you export a drawing, animated GIF, screen recording, or timelapse, WobblyPaint 95 saves it straight to your device's Photos app, inside a "WobblyPaint 95" album.

The app can't save to my Photos library

iOS: Settings > WobblyPaint 95 > Photos > set to "Add Photos Only" or "All Photos".

Android: Settings > Apps > WobblyPaint 95 > Permissions > allow Photos/Media access.

Can I use layers, custom brushes, and undo/redo?

Yes — WobblyPaint 95 supports multiple layers, a range of brushes (including spray and bucket fill), shape and line tools with an optional grid, and full undo/redo history.

Does WobblyPaint 95 require an account or internet connection?

No. WobblyPaint 95 works fully offline and doesn't require signing in or creating an account.
